Success Rates - Fertility Plus - Harley Street Fertility Clinic
Our success rates...
Fertility Plus follows a new concept in obtaining exceptionally high pregnancy rates by introducing freezing of embryos and their transfer either after genetic screening of embryos or without them.
Frozen Eggs
Clinical pregnancy rates (fetal heart detected) under the age of 37 (age at which eggs are frozen).
Success Rate:
50%
Frozen Embryo Transfer
Clinical pregnancy rates (presence of positive fetal heart at 7 weeks). Frozen embryo transfer gives the best results and in general is a favoured approach.
<37 years:
63%
37-39 years:
50%
40-41 years:
25%
