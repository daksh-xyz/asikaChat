Fertility Counselling
Counselling is a vital part of fertility treatment and Mr. Gudi and Mr. Shah offer the services of a
specialist fertility
counsellor before, during and after the fertility treatments. Some treatments, such as donor treatments, legally require counselling according to the HFEA code of practice.
Mr Gudi and Mr Shah will discuss counselling options with you during your consultation.
